This mod is developed purely for entertainment, developed by me alone, and still under development. 
When playing with the Calamity mod, some content will be adjusted to fit Calamity's numerical environment.

Currently, this mod includes the following series:
- Stary Sword series, Stary Sniper Rifle series, and Stary Staff series - a total of 30 Stary weapons
- Stary Armor series with 10 sets, Full Moon Emblem series with 10 Emblems, and Stary Emblem series with 10 Emblems
(Art assets may be reused due to limited artistic skills)

Additionally, there are:
- Nearly 20 other types of weapons, 6 accessories, 3 ingots, 1 armor set, and 1 incomplete boss
This mod does not rewrite existing boss AI, so it allows joining after creating the mod
If you have any ideas or suggestions, feel free to leave a comment.
